Course details

Who can come?

Creative teenagers from grade 10 - 12.

We recommend that students start Putter School at the beginning of Grade 10, but Grade 11 and 12 students will also find the course empowering.

Where?

Independently on the Rondebosch Boys’ High School campus.

When?

Once a week during term-time (Tues, Wed or Thur).
16:00 - 18:00

Classes are on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Students choose which one of these days to attend Putter School for the year. Choice of day is on a first come first served basis. There are no classes during National Department of Education school holidays.

How much?

R1,235 a month
R14,820 a year

Minimum enrolment is for a year, with the option to continue until the end of matric. Basic materials are included in the fee.

What do you love making? The best way to build a solid future for yourself is to identify and develop your creative strengths when you’re still young. At Putter School you’ll be exposed to a wide range of creative activities to help you discover what you really want to do with your life.

Payment details

Secure a place with a deposit.

Entrance to Putter School is limited. Once the school is full by the beginning of 2016, very few places may open up for the following three years.

Entrance to Putter School is on a first come first served basis. The only way to ensure a place is to pay a non-refundable deposit of R1,482 – which is 10% of the total fee. The deposit will be subtracted from the total amount owing.

It is possible to enrol at Putter School without paying a deposit, unless all places have already been filled by deposit-paying students.

Yearly fee minus deposit = R11,865
Termly fee minus deposit = R3,148 per term
Monthly fee minus deposit = R1,111 per month
