Course details

Who can come?

Creative teenagers from grade 9 - 12.

Older and younger students may also attend but only by prior arrangement.

Where?

Independently on the Rondebosch Boys’ High School campus.

When?

Once a week during term-time (Tues, Wed or Thur).
16:00 - 18:00

Classes are on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Students choose one of these days to attend Putter School, which they can change whenever they want or need to. Putter School usually starts a week later in January - and ends a week earlier in December - than the National Department of Education start and end times. Holidays usually coincide with National Department of Education school holidays.

How much?

R1,490 a month
R16,135 a year

Minimum enrolment is for a year, with the option to continue until the end of matric. Basic materials are included in the fee.

What do you love making? The best way to build a solid future for yourself is to identify and develop your creative strengths when you’re still young. At Putter School you’ll be exposed to a wide range of creative activities to help you discover what you really want to do with your life.

How to secure a place at Putter School

If you are interested in attending Putter School, please fill out the Get In Touch form here. After submitting the form, Andrew will be in touch to answer any questions you might have and to let you know if there are any places available. You can then decide if you’d like to join us!
To secure a place, you will be asked to pay a non-refundable deposit of R1,235. The deposit will be subtracted from the total amount owing.
